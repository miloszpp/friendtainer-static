  • Basics

  • What is Friendtainer?

    Friendtainer is an app which helps you maintain relationships. It lets you define how often you want to meet with your friends. Whenever you meet with one of your friends, you record a meeting in Friendtainer. If you fail to meet with your friends according to the desired frequency, Friendtainer will send you gentle reminders via email or push notifications.

  • How to add a friend to Friendtainer?

    Tap the menu symbol, then tap My Friends. Next find the plus symbol and tap it. Fill in the friend's name and decide how often would you like to meet. You can also decide whether you want to receive meeting reminders for this particular friend.

  • How to log a meeting?

    Tap on the friend you want to log meeting for. Tap the Record meeting button and enter the date of the meeting. Tap on Save meeting.

    Show instruction (animated GIF)

  • How to delete a contact?

    Slide to the left the list item representing the contact you want to delete. Confirm by clicking on the red button.

  • Reminders

  • How to enable reminders?

    You can enable email reminders and push reminders in the settings pane. Just clicking on the settings button and toggle either Push notifications or Emails. Email reminders are enabled by default. If you try to enable push notifications, your device may ask you for permission to receive notifications. You need to approve if you want to get notified.

    Show instruction (animated GIF)

  • When will I receive reminders?

    You start receiving daily reminders if you don't record a subsequent meeting after desired period of time. For example, if you choose to meet with a friend weekly, you will start receiving reminders 7 days after the previous meeting.

  • Why I cannot enable push notifications?

    Although Friendtainer runs in a browser, you can choose to receive push notifications just as if you used a native mobile app. Currently push notifications will only work on modern Android phones. You can enable push notifications in the menu. Push notifications currenly work only on one device at a time. If you logout from a device, push notifications will be disabled.

  • Miscellaneous

  • Is my data safe?

    Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers and protected by Firebase authentication.

    Friendtainer enforces HTTPS protocol making the transfer of your data secure.
